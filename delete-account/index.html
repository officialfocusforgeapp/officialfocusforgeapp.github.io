Delete Account

This page explains how to request deletion of eligible cloud-backed FocusForge account data and what information may need to be retained.

Last updated: April 9, 2026

On this page

  • 1. How to request deletion
  • 2. What to include
  • 3. What may be retained

1. How to request deletion

To request deletion of eligible cloud-backed account data, email officialfocusforgeapp@gmail.com from the email associated with your account, or include enough identifying information for the request to be matched to the correct record.

Put “FocusForge Deletion Request” in the subject line to make the request easier to route.

2. What to include

  • Your account email or another identifier associated with the account.
  • Your display name or profile name, if relevant.
  • A brief statement confirming that you want your eligible cloud-backed account data deleted.
  • Any details needed to identify linked partner or family records if applicable.

3. What may be retained

FocusForge may retain information where necessary for legal compliance, billing records, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, security, dispute resolution, or enforcement of agreements.

Local data stored only on your device may also need to be removed by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through Android settings.

4. Subscriptions and platform purchases

Deleting eligible cloud-backed account data does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription. Platform purchases generally must be managed through your Google Play account and subscription settings.

For billing and refund guidance, see Support.

5. Contact

Deletion requests and related questions can be sent to officialfocusforgeapp@gmail.com.

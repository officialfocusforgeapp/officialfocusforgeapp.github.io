How to request deletion

Email from the account email associated with FocusForge, or clearly identify that account email in the request.

  • Subject line: Delete FocusForge account
  • Include the account email used in the app
  • State that you want cloud-backed account data deleted

Important notes

Deletion processing may require enough information to verify the request and locate the relevant cloud-backed data. Some records may need to be retained where required by law, platform obligations, fraud prevention, or transaction support.

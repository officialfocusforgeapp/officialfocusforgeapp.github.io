This page explains how to request deletion of cloud-backed account data. Local on-device data may need to be removed separately through Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

1. How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of a FocusForge cloud-backed account, email officialfocusforgeapp@gmail.com using the subject line FocusForge Account Deletion Request.

Include
Your account email address, username if available, and a clear statement that you want your FocusForge account deleted.
Optional
Any additional information that helps verify ownership of the account you want removed.

2. What Gets Deleted

  • Cloud-backed account profile information and account metadata associated with the account.
  • Linked family-control, accountability, or relationship records stored for that account where technically associated with the deletion request.
  • Remote settings, sync records, and related cloud data tied to the account, subject to legal and operational retention limits.

3. What May Remain

  • Local on-device data stays on your device until you separately clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • Limited records may be retained for security, fraud prevention, dispute handling, accounting, backup integrity, or legal compliance.
  • Residual data may persist temporarily in logs, backups, or archived systems before normal deletion cycles complete.

4. Local Device Data

Deleting a cloud-backed account does not automatically erase data stored locally on your device. To remove local app data, use the app’s data controls if available, clear local app storage in Android settings, or uninstall the app.

5. Processing and Verification

Deletion requests are reviewed manually. FocusForge may ask for reasonable verification before deleting cloud-backed data to help prevent unauthorized deletion requests.

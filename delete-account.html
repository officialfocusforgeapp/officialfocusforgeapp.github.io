FocusForge Account Deletion

Effective date: March 12, 2026

This page explains how a FocusForge user can request deletion of their account and related cloud data.

Delete request contact

Email officialfocusforgeapp@gmail.com with the subject line FocusForge Account Deletion Request.

1. What to send

  • Your FocusForge account email address.
  • Your FocusForge username, if available.
  • A statement that you want your FocusForge account deleted.

2. What gets deleted

  • Your cloud-backed account profile and account metadata.
  • Linked family-control/accountability relationships stored for that account.
  • Remote settings and related cloud records associated with that account, subject to operational and legal retention limits.

3. What may remain

  • Local on-device data stays on the device until the user also deletes local app data or uninstalls the app.
  • Limited records may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, dispute handling, or legal compliance.

4. Local data deletion

Users can delete local on-device FocusForge data from the app’s Privacy screen. This is separate from cloud account deletion.

5. Processing

Deletion requests are reviewed manually. If we need to verify account ownership before deleting cloud data, we may reply to confirm the request.

Privacy

Privacy that is direct, permission-aware, and built for a focus app.

This Privacy Policy explains how FocusForge collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the FocusForge app and related services.

Effective date
March 11, 2026

FocusForge

This policy is written to match the current FocusForge feature set and related site pages. Update it again before launch if your data flows, SDKs, ads, subscriptions, or account systems materially change.

Summary

  • FocusForge uses Android Accessibility only to detect the foreground app package name so blocking can work for apps you choose to control.
  • FocusForge does not use Accessibility to read typed text, messages, media content, or general screen contents.
  • Usage Access is optional and is used only for app-time insights, projections, and related productivity features where enabled.
  • You can disable permissions in Android settings, modify your app configuration in-app, or request deletion of cloud-backed account data.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, FocusForge may collect or process:

  • Account and profile data: username, internal user ID, account role, profile settings, and app preferences.
  • Blocking and configuration data: app blocking rules, schedules, challenge settings, severity choices, and feature configuration.
  • Feature event data: blocked app attempts, focus session activity, achievement events, challenge status, and related app-function records.
  • Accessibility-related data: the foreground app package name currently open on your device for rule matching and blocking.
  • Optional Usage Access data: per-app foreground duration statistics exposed by Android Usage Stats APIs.
  • Optional family, accountability, or social data: linked relationships, partner or family settings, and metadata needed to run those workflows.
  • Monetization data when enabled: if ads or subscriptions are enabled, related billing or ad event data may be processed by the relevant providers under their own policies.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide app blocking, focus sessions, progression systems, accountability features, and related core functionality.
  • Generate optional app-time insights and projections when Usage Access is enabled by the user.
  • Maintain account relationships such as family or accountability systems when those features are used.
  • Support security, anti-abuse, troubleshooting, diagnostics, product improvement, and service reliability.
  • Operate billing, entitlement, and monetization systems when subscriptions or ad-supported features are enabled.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable, FocusForge may process information under one or more of these legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract to provide the service features you request.
  • Legitimate interests, such as security, fraud prevention, reliability, service improvement, and abuse prevention, where those interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent where required, such as optional permissions or optional features.
  • Compliance with legal obligations and lawful requests.

4. Where Data Is Stored

Primary app data may be stored locally on your device using app storage systems. If cloud-backed features are enabled, relevant account, sync, social, accountability, or subscription-related data may also be stored with configured service providers such as Firebase or other infrastructure providers used for FocusForge.

5. Sharing of Information

FocusForge does not sell personal data. Information may be shared only as reasonably necessary to operate the service, such as with:

  • Cloud providers used for authentication, data storage, account features, sync, or diagnostics.
  • Billing or ad partners when subscriptions, purchases, or ad-supported features are enabled.
  • Legal or regulatory authorities where required by law, valid process, or to protect rights, safety, or the service.

6. Data Retention

  • Local device data generally remains until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud-backed account data may be retained while the relevant account or feature remains active, subject to operational, security, fraud-prevention, accounting, and legal retention needs.
  • Some records may persist for a limited period in backups, logs, or security systems.

7. Your Choices and Controls

  • You can disable Accessibility or Usage Access in Android settings.
  • You can change or remove many configurations from within the app.
  • You can request deletion of cloud-backed account data through the delete-account page.
  • You can remove local data from your device separately from cloud account deletion.

8. Children and Family Controls

FocusForge may include family-control, parent/child, or accountability-oriented features. Parents and guardians are responsible for lawful use of those features and for obtaining any permissions or consent required for child accounts or monitored usage.

9. Security

FocusForge uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to protect information. No method of transmission, storage, or security control is perfect, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10. International Transfers

If FocusForge uses cloud providers that operate infrastructure across multiple regions, information may be processed or stored in jurisdictions outside your home region, subject to provider practices and applicable law.

11. Changes to This Policy

FocusForge may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by revising the effective date or last updated date on this page.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or account deletion requests, contact officialfocusforgeapp@gmail.com.

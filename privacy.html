FocusForge Privacy Policy

Privacy Terms

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, processing, storage, and disclosure of information by FocusForge ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") in connection with the FocusForge mobile application and related services (the "Service").

2. Categories of Information Collected

Account Information

  • Username, internal identifiers, role designation (self/parent/child).
  • Profile preferences and configuration data.

Blocking Configuration & Activity Events

  • User-defined rules, schedules, severity configurations.
  • System-generated logs (e.g., blocked app events, focus sessions).

Accessibility Data

  • Foreground application package identifier solely for rule enforcement.
  • No keystrokes, no screen content capture, no message or media inspection.

Optional Usage Access

  • Aggregated per-application duration statistics via Android Usage APIs.

Commercial & Advertising Data

  • Advertising interaction metadata where monetization features are enabled.
  • Billing data processed exclusively via Google Play infrastructure.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract (service delivery).
  • Legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention, product improvement).
  • User consent where required.
  • Legal compliance obligations.

4. Data Storage & Security

Primary application data is stored locally on user devices. Where cloud features are enabled, data may be processed through secure Firebase infrastructure. Industry-standard technical and administrative safeguards are implemented.

5. International Transfers

Data may be processed in jurisdictions where our infrastructure providers operate. Standard contractual safeguards are applied where required by applicable law.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for operational, contractual, or legal purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, users may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port personal data. Requests may be submitted to:

8. California Privacy Rights (CPRA)

California residents may request disclosure of categories of personal information collected, request deletion, and opt-out of sale/sharing. FocusForge does not sell personal data.

9. Children's Privacy

Parent/guardian authorization is required for minor accounts. The Service does not knowingly collect personal information from children without lawful consent.

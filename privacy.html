This Privacy Policy describes how FocusForge (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, processes, and protects information in connection with the FocusForge mobile application and related services (“Service”).

  • Username, internal identifiers, and role classification (Self / Parent / Child).
  • Profile settings and configuration preferences.

Blocking & Usage Configuration

  • User-defined app blocking rules, schedules, severity settings.
  • Event logs (e.g., blocked attempts, focus sessions, achievements).

Commercial & Advertising Data

  • Advertising interaction metadata where ad-supported features are enabled.

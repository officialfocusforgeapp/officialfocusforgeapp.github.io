Privacy Policy

How FocusForge handles data and permissions.

This summary is written for clarity and public review. It is designed to stay consistent with the product disclosures shown across the site.

What the app needs to function

FocusForge uses Accessibility so it can detect which app is in the foreground and apply the blocking rules selected by the user. It does not use Accessibility to read typed text, messages, media, or general screen contents.

Usage Access is optional and may be used for app-time insights and related diagnostics when enabled.

What data may be stored

Depending on enabled features, FocusForge may store rule selections, session activity, blocked attempts, account-linked settings, and cloud-backed data tied to configured providers such as Firebase and Google Play billing.

User controls

Users can review policies, manage permissions in Android settings, and request deletion of cloud-backed account data. The Delete Account page provides the live public route for those requests.

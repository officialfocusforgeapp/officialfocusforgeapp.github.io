1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains how FocusForge handles billing questions, cancellations, and refund requests for digital purchases and subscriptions.

2. Purchases Through App Stores

If you purchase FocusForge through a third-party app store such as Google Play, that platform may control payment processing, billing methods, taxes, cancellation mechanics, and certain refund decisions. In many cases, you must request refunds through the same store used to make the purchase.

3. Subscription Cancellations

  • You can generally cancel a subscription at any time through the platform used to purchase it.
  • Canceling usually stops future renewals but does not automatically create a refund for charges already processed.
  • Access to paid features may continue until the end of the current billing period unless stated otherwise by the purchase platform.

4. Refund Requests

Where FocusForge directly controls a charge, refund requests may be considered on a case-by-case basis, including for duplicate charges, technical entitlement failures, or clear billing mistakes. Refunds are not guaranteed except where required by law or platform policy.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Except where required by law, refunds may be denied for situations such as:

  • Failure to cancel before renewal.
  • Change of mind after a digital feature has been delivered or used.
  • Device incompatibility or configuration issues outside FocusForge’s control that were apparent before purchase.
  • Violations of the Usage Terms or abuse of promotional offers.

6. How to Request Help

For billing questions or support, contact officialfocusforgeapp@gmail.com and include the purchase email, transaction identifier if available, platform used, and a short description of the issue.

7. Changes

FocusForge may update this Refund Policy by revising the last updated date on this page.

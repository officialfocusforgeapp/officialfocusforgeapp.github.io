Copyright

A clean process for copyright notices and counter-notices.

This page explains how copyright owners can submit infringement notices and how counter-notices may be handled.

Effective date
March 11, 2026
App
FocusForge
Contact
officialfocusforgeapp@gmail.com

1. Copyright Complaints

If you believe material made available through FocusForge infringes your copyright, you may send a notice containing the information required by applicable law, including the DMCA where applicable.

2. What to Include in a Notice

  • Your full name and contact information.
  • A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed.
  • A description of the allegedly infringing material and where it appears.
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury where applicable, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

3. Where to Send Notices

Send copyright notices to officialfocusforgeapp@gmail.com with the subject line DMCA Notice.

4. Counter-Notices

If you believe content was removed or disabled by mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notice containing information required by law. FocusForge may forward valid counter-notices to the original complainant and may restore the material where allowed.

5. Repeat Infringement

FocusForge may suspend or terminate accounts of repeat infringers where appropriate.

6. Good-Faith Use Only

Submitting knowingly false infringement claims or counter-notices can create legal liability. Please send notices only when you have a good-faith basis for doing so.

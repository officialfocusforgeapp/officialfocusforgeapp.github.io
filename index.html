FAQ

Direct answers.

What does FocusForge actually block?

It is built for app blocking, website blocking, saved schedules, and time-boxed Lock In sessions on Android.

What is Addict Mode or Risk Group for?

It is for users who need stricter behavior during high-risk periods, with harder exits and more serious unblock friction.

Does it read what I type or what is on my screen?

No. Accessibility is used to identify which app is in the foreground so the rules you chose can be applied. It is not used to read typed content, messages, or general screen contents.

Is Usage Access required?

No. Usage Access is optional and powers app-time insights when enabled.

Can it work for couples or families?

Yes. FocusForge is designed for solo use, partner accountability, and household controls with linked child devices.

How do subscriptions and refunds work?

Subscriptions may be sold through Google Play. Billing, renewals, and many refund outcomes are controlled by Google Play and its store policies.

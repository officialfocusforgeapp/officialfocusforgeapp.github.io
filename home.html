Simple setup. High leverage. The goal is to make your default behavior better, not make productivity feel like homework.

1

2

🔒

Permission clarity

Accessibility is used to detect the foreground app package so blocking can work for the apps you choose. It is not used to read typed content, messages, or general screen contents.

⚙️

Control stays with you

You can disable permissions in Android settings, change your app rules in-app, request deletion of cloud-backed account data, or remove local app data from your device.

☁️

Cloud features when enabled

Some account, sync, accountability, or monetization features may rely on configured providers such as Firebase or ad services when those systems are enabled.

Common questions

Quick answers to the questions users are most likely to ask.

What does FocusForge actually do?

FocusForge is built to help users reduce distraction by applying configurable app blocking, focus systems, and visible progression mechanics that make consistency easier to maintain.

Does FocusForge need special permissions?

Some blocking and usage features may require Android permissions such as accessibility or usage access. Those permissions are only used for the features you enable and are explained in the Privacy Policy.

Can users request account deletion?

Yes. The site includes a dedicated delete-account page with request instructions, plus linked privacy and support pages for related questions.

Where should support or legal notices be sent?

General support, privacy questions, deletion requests, refund questions, and copyright notices can be sent to officialfocusforgeapp@gmail.com.

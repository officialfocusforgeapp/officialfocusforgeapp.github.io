2. Best Path by Request Type

•
General app support: describe the issue, device, Android version, and steps to reproduce it.
•
Privacy request: include the account email and clearly explain the request.
•
Account deletion: use the subject line FocusForge Account Deletion Request.
•
Billing question: include the purchase email, platform, and transaction reference if available.
•
DMCA notice: use the subject line DMCA Notice and include the information required by law.

4. What to Include

Clear requests get handled faster. Include your account email, the issue you are contacting about, and any supporting information that helps identify the request. For technical issues, include your device model and Android version where relevant.

5. Important Notes

For account deletion, deleting cloud-backed account data does not always remove local app data stored on the device. Local data may need to be removed separately through Android settings or by uninstalling the app.
